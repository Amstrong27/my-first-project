OUR STORY

Our Story

Since 1984, Folham has used its outdoor advertising expertise to help companies of every size reach out to their current and potential customers. To compete in the fast growing industry, we keep modernizing our operations by introducing new products offerings in the OOH space, these products range from Digital LED Billboards, to "Human Walking" billboards which are popular for creating awareness for brands during activations and other formal /informal events.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative and efficient advertising solutions that elevate your brand's visibility. To compete in the fast growing industry, we keep modernizing our operations by introducing new products offerings in the OOH space, these products range from Digital LED Billboards, to "Human Walking" billboards which are popular for creating awareness for brands during activations and other formal /informal events.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to continue leading the transformation of outdoor advertising in Nigeria by embracing innovation and technology, helping brands build stronger connections with their audiences, and driving impactful results.
