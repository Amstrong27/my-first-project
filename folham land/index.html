WHO WE ARE

Since 1984, Folham has used its outdoor advertising expertise to help companies of every size reach out to their current and potential customers. To compete in the fast growing industry, we keep modernizing our operations by introducing new products offerings in the OOH space, these products range from Digital LED Billboards, to "Human Walking" billboards which are popular for creating awareness for brands during activations and other formal /informal events.

We also actively participate in providing publicly owned OOH assets like; Public Buses (BRT), Bus Shelters and Street Lamppost to our existing clients in various parts of the country.
