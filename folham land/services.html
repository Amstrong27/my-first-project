Billboard Advertisement

Our extensive network of billboards is strategically placed in high-traffic areas across Nigeria. From major highways to busy urban centers, our billboards ensure maximum exposure for your brand. 02/

Lamppost Advertisement

Lamppost advertising is a captivating and an attractive way of presenting brand messages. These messages display for 24hours and most times the streetlights on which these materials are mounted on create a source of illumination for the materials making the messages visible at night; sometimes an internal lighting system is fitted in for that same purpose as well. Lamppost advertising is very effective in creating awareness about some social and political issues.

03/

Transit Advertisement

Transit advertising on buses, cars, taxi, bus stops and any other means of public transportation and buildings has been an effective tool to increase brand awareness to diverse audience across strategic routes. They are cost effective, captivating weather resistant and offers unlimited exposure. Consumers are reached at the right time, commuters and pedestrians see the advert when they commute to and from work daily.

Branding and Activations

Activation is a marketing event used by brands to help position their brand into the consumers’ minds and this can be done through product sampling, in-store retail marketing and experiential events; this is the perfect way to reach potential consumers in order to shift perceptions and create a real emotional engagement. Using our various planning and execution skills, we implement brand activation campaigns for our clients creating visibility for them and direct brand affinity with their target customers.
